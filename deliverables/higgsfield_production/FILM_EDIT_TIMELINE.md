## Music & Sound Direction

Per `design/10_HIGGSFIELD_PREP.md`: no autoplay audio, ever. If a scored version is produced for paid media or a homepage hero placement, use a single continuous minimal piano/ambient cue with no percussion hits synced to cuts — the edit's own pacing (fast in Act 2, slow in Act 3, warm in Act 5) should carry the emotion, not a music sting. If ambient location sound is layered in, keep it under the score: garden birdsong under Act 3, distant rooftop conversation murmur under Act 5, nothing under Acts 1, 2, 4, or 6.

## Colour & Delivery Notes

- Grade to match the existing colour-corrected `site/public/media` source throughout — no new grading pass introduced at the edit stage.
- Deliver at minimum 1080p24 for web/social; 4K master if the source-image upscale supports it cleanly (verify no artifacting before committing to a 4K deliverable).
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 master; a 9:16 vertical recut is a separate future deliverable, not covered by this timeline, since several shots (bedroom, kitchen, reception) were composed and shot list-approved for horizontal framing only.
