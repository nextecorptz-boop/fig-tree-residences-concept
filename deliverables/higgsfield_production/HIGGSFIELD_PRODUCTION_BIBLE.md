**Production database:** `animations/higgsfield_shot_list.json` (source of truth for source images, durations, and movement codes)

**Shot count:** 37
**Total runtime (raw shots, before edit trims):** 226 seconds

## Global Production Rules

1. The photograph is the truth. The camera reveals it; it never rewrites it.
2. No shot exceeds 8 seconds.
3. No shot invents architecture, furniture, decor, fixtures, or people not present in the source photograph.
4. Approved camera moves only: Dolly In, Dolly Out, Crane Up, Crane Down, Arc Left, Arc Right, Focus Pull, Hero Cam, Slow Handheld, 360 Orbit, Pan, Tilt. No chaotic or handheld-shake motion.
5. Lens discipline follows `design/10_HIGGSFIELD_PREP.md`: standard 35–50mm equivalents for interiors and comparability across rooms; slightly longer (50–85mm) for intimate garden and detail shots; wide (28mm) only for exteriors and the pool deck, never fisheye.
6. Grading matches the existing colour-corrected `site/public/media` source — no re-introduction of the original yellow-green cast.
7. Sound, if used at all in the final edit, is optional, muted by default, and never autoplays.
