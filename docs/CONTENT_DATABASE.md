# Fig Tree Residences — Content Database
### Phase 1A: Discovery & Asset Intelligence

This document is a verbatim record of every piece of marketing copy, contact detail, and structured fact recovered from the printed brochure photographs and the live website. It is a raw source file for later copywriting, not a finished narrative — quotations are transcribed exactly as they appear, including the inconsistencies and one apparent misspelling noted below.
